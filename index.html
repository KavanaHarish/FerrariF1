The most iconic team in Formula 1 history

Scuderia Ferrari is the oldest and most successful team in Formula 1. Founded in 1929 by Enzo Ferrari, the team has built some of the most iconic racing cars in motorsport history.

About Ferrari


Scuderia Ferrari is the racing division of Ferrari and competes in the Formula One World Championship. The team was founded by Enzo Ferrari and made its Formula One debut in 1950.

Ferrari is famous for its iconic red racing cars and its passionate fan base known as the Tifosi. Over decades, Ferrari has produced some of the greatest drivers and most successful racing cars in motorsport history.

Ferrari's Legacy

Ferrari has a rich history in Formula 1, with legendary drivers like Michael Schumacher, Niki Lauda, and Sebastian Vettel contributing to the team's success.

Ferrari's Achievements

Ferrari has won the Constructors' Championship a record 16 times and the Drivers' Championship 15 times.
